About Arch De Arch

Our Story

ARCH DE ARCH Company Limited is a limited liability company registered under the laws of Ghana. We serve the advertising, branding and corporate merchandising needs of companies within and around Ghana.

Our Mission

Our drive is to build and improve our clients' image and rise above market competitiveness. We believe spreading the brand and image of companies is the best way to get your Products or Services stuck in the minds of people. We offer audio-visual productions, 2D and 3D animations and Social Media management and advertising.
